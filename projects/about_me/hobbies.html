I have recently built my first P.C. back in spring 2020. I plan on building another P.C. in the next few years. I learned from YouTube videos and P.C. Part Picker. Everything went together without any issues and I learned a lot about what each component does. I highly recommend building your own P.C. for learning about computers as well as save you some money. The parts I used are the following...

  • Rog Strix B-450f motherboard
  • AMD Ryzen 5 3600 Processor
  • 16GB of DDR4 R.A.M.
  • 500GB M.2 SSD
  • ROG Strix RTX 2060 Graphics Card
  • 650watt Power Unit
  • Phanteks P-300 Case (white)
  • Plently of RGB :)
