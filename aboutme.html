About me

Hello, My name is Brett Wolfangel. I am from St. Louis, Mo. I attended Oakville Senior High School and graduated in 2012. After high school, I attended St. Louis Community College from 2012-2015. In 2015, I applied to Missouri State University and studied business. I struggled through business classes at MSU and decided to finish an associates degree at Ozarks Technical Community College. I graduated in the summer of 2019. I entered the job market in the fall of 2019 and could not find my dream job. I decided to look into OTC's Computer Science program. I started back into school in summer of 2020. I enrolled in a web development class and found my passion. My future plans for school are to finish my degree at OTC within a year and enter back into the job market.
The computer classes I have taken so far are CIS-201 (Computer Apps for Business) and CIS-130 (Web Development I). In the fall semester, I plan on taking Web Development II, Intro to Computer Programming, C#, and Systems Analysis and Design.
